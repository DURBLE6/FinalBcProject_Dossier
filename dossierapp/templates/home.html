ABOUT DOSSIER

Dossier is a web application designed for corporate organisations to serve as a tool that helps the top tier employees of an organisation communicate with the management. It allows top officers (Managers, Supervisors, etc) to generate and send a report, typically regarding the ethics and behaviour of an individual worker towards duties and assignments in the organisation. Dossier ensures swift and prompt communications as each message or report will be attended to and the sender gets notified almost immediately
